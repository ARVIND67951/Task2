Q1. What is VPS Hosting?

A virtual private server (VPS) hosting is basically a virtually isolated server. It creates a completely isolated environment for your websites and applications, allowing you to manage your server resources freely and giving you full control over your server. It comes with full root access and dedicated resources, making it more powerful than shared hosting. However, it's not as powerful as a dedicated server but still a great option for businesses looking for a reliable, scalable, and secure hosting solution.
